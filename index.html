This is my home page for all of my csc 196w projects.

One thing I found about today is a project called uniwave.
A company called Wave Swells has created an artificial blow hole that uses waves to create energy.
As a wave comes it pushes air out of the blow hole and then when the wave receeds, air is sucked in through as turbine to create energy.
As everything is above the water it is easy to work on and less harmful to the environment.
